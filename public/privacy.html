Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-10

This policy explains how AuPair Rematch collects and uses data for matching, messaging, and safety operations.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect account identifiers (UID, email), profile details you enter, matching actions, chat content, safety/abuse reports, and product feedback.

2. Why We Process Data

We process data to provide matching features, maintain platform safety, investigate abuse reports, improve product quality, and operate the service reliably.

3. Legal Basis and Consent

You must be at least 18 years old and accept Terms and Privacy Policy before sign-in. Consent metadata is recorded in your user account document.

4. Sharing

We do not publicly reveal your identity by default. Data may be accessed by authorized administrators for moderation, support, and legal compliance.

5. Retention

We retain account and activity data while your account is active. Safety reports and feedback may be retained for moderation history. You may request account deletion, after which your profile is hidden and queued for processing.

6. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data. Use in-app deletion request or contact the service administrator.

7. Security

We use Firebase Authentication, Firestore rules, and hosting security headers to protect data, but no system is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8. Contact

For privacy requests about this community help project, please use the in-app feedback form (Profile → Send feedback to admin). Requests are reviewed by the project administrator.

9. Updates

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by a new effective date.
